Privacy Policy

Got Health? allows you to read information from the Apple Health App in a user friendly format. The app can only read information from the Apple Health App after you give it specific permission.

The Got Health? app does not communicate with or share data with any party, including the developer. You do not need to register or share any information with anyone to use this app. The app does allow you to send your Health information as a "csv" file to anyone you choose via email. This email can only be sent to an email address YOU select.
